Integrating Mission AnalysisÂ¶

You can perform aerodynamic or aerostructural wing design optimization considering full mission analysis by integrating OpenAeroStruct with OpenConcept. OpenConcept is a toolkit for aircraft conceptual design, which is also built on top of the OpenMDAO framework.

Examples of the OpenAeroStruct-OpenConcept coupled work can be found in OpenConcept repository and documentation.
